Migrate My Reminder

Migrate iOS Reminders between iCloud accounts — offline, private, and simple.

What it does

  • Extract reminders from your current iCloud account
  • Review extracted lists and items before importing
  • Import into a new iCloud account, preserving lists or merging into one
  • Works entirely offline; your data stays on device

How it works

  • Select an extraction date range (by due date) or all reminders
  • Extract reminders and review them by list and month
  • Switch iCloud account on device
  • Import: preserve original lists or import into a single list

Privacy

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device. No analytics. No ads. No trackers.
